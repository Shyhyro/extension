◀ Tagline Formatting Legend


↗️ Open in new tab

Use these placeholders to compose a tagline format for your horses. Go to the main page of this popout menu and replace the value under "Tagline Generator" to autosave what you've written.

Conformation

  • {vg} Very Good
  • {gs} Good+
  • {g} Good
  • {a} Average
  • {ba} Below Average
  • {p} Poor

Scores

  • {hs} Highest Visible
  • {ls} Lowest Visible

Append _r0 through _r3 to round a score's decimal places. e.g., ls_r1 will round a score like 79.973 to 80.0.

Genetic Potential

  • {gp} Genetic Potential
  • {dr} Dressage
  • {dv} Driving
  • {en} Endurance
  • {ev} Eventing
  • {rc} Flat Racing
  • {sj} Show Jumping
  • {re} Western Reining

Percentages

  • {drp} Dressage %
  • {dvp} Driving %
  • {enp} Endurance %
  • {evp} Eventing %
  • {rcp} Flat Racing %
  • {sjp} Show Jumping %
  • {rep} Western Reining %
