Tagline Formatting Legend

Use these placeholders to compose a tagline format for your horses. Go to the popout menu and replace the value under "Tagline Generator" to autosave what you've written.

Conformation

  • {vg} Very Good
  • {gs} Good+
  • {g} Good
  • {ag} All Goods (g + gs)
  • {a} Average
  • {ba} Below Average
  • {p} Poor

Individual Stats

  • {wal} Walk
  • {trt} Trot
  • {can} Canter
  • {gal} Gallop
  • {pos} Posture
  • {hea} Head
  • {nck} Neck
  • {bck} Back
  • {sld} Shoulders
  • {flg} Frontlegs
  • {hdq} Hindquarters
  • {sck} Socks

Conformation Stats in Discipline Summaries

  • {dr_g} Dressage
  • {dv_g} Driving
  • {en_g} Endurance
  • {ev_g} Eventing
  • {rc_g} Flat racing
  • {sj_g} Show jumping
  • {re_g} Western reining

Use g for Good or replace it with gs, ag, or vg to use the values for Good+, All Goods (g + gs), or Very Good, respectively.

Scores

  • {hs} Highest Visible
  • {ls} Lowest Visible
  • {rng} Score Range (adds 6.062 to ls if the horse is an Icelandic, else 6.928)

Append _r0 through _r2 to round a score's decimal places. e.g., ls_r1 will round a score like 79.973 to 80.0.

Append _r0 or _r1 to round a percentage's decimal places. e.g., drp_r1 will round a percentage like 64.25 to 64.3.

  • {sex_u} F, C, S, M, or G
  • {ln} Lifenumber

Breed Total

  • {bt} Breed Total (overall GP)
  • {dr_bt} Breed Total (Dressage)
  • {dv_bt} Breed Total (Driving)
  • {en_bt} Breed Total (Endurance)
  • {ev_bt} Breed Total (Eventing)
  • {rc_bt} Breed Total (Flat Racing)
  • {sj_bt} Breed Total (Show Jumping)
  • {re_bt} Breed Total (Western Reining)
  • {dr_obt} Overall Breed Total (Dressage)
  • {dv_obt} Overall Breed Total (Driving)
  • {en_obt} Overall Breed Total (Endurance)
  • {ev_obt} Overall Breed Total (Eventing)
  • {rc_obt} Overall Breed Total (Flat Racing)
  • {sj_obt} Overall Breed Total (Show Jumping)
  • {re_obt} Overall Breed Total (Western Reining)
Breed Total Formulas
  • Breed total: ((GP / 10) + HighestScore) / 2
  • Discipline breed total: ((DisciplineGP / NumberOfTraits) + HighestScore) / 2
  • Overall breed total: ((GP / 10) + HighestScore + (DisciplineGP / NumberOfTraits)) / 3

Append _r0 or _r1 to round a total's decimal places. e.g., dr_bt_r1 will round a score like 53.38 to 53.4.
